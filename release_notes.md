MikuBot has transcended into its most powerful visual form yet! This version represents a massive technical leap in how your assistant communicates complex ideas. **v2.2.0** is the definitive "Scientist & Scholar Update," introducing a high-performance rendering engine that brings professional-grade technical precision to your desktop.

### ✨ What's New in This Release?

*   **🧪 Advanced LaTeX Math Engine:** For the first time, MikuBot can render high-fidelity mathematical formulas. From multiline calculus ($$) and matrices to quantum physics constants (\hbar, \oint) and statistics, everything is now displayed as beautiful, publication-quality Unicode/HTML structures.
*   **📂 Obsidian-Style Collapsible Callouts:** Total organization for complex thoughts. We've implemented Obsidian-like syntax for callouts (`> [!TYPE]-` for collapsed, `> [!TYPE]+` for expanded) with premium rotating indicators and tailored indigo-themed aesthetics.
*   **⌨️ Synchronized Typing Animations:** Experience a truly "alive" interface. Progressive text reveal (typing effect) is now fully integrated into callouts, blockquotes, and lists, perfectly synchronized with the streaming neural engine for a cohesive, cinematic response feel.
*   **🛡️ Asset Protection Pipeline (3-Phase):** We overhauled the internal Markdown pipeline. Images, links, and code blocks are now shielded in a 3-phase process, preventing structural corruption and ensuring 100% reliable rendering of nested assets and technical documentation.
*   **💠 Premium Scientific Visualization:** Math containers now feature subtle glassmorphism and center-alignment, while inline math gets a distinctive amber-200 highlight, making technical reports as beautiful as they are accurate.

### 📦 How to Update Safely

Simply download the new installer **MikuBot-Setup-2.2.0.exe** from the **Assets** section below and run it. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Safety First:** The wizard will handle the transition of your workspace folders. However, as this update introduces major rendering changes, we strongly recommend: **Run the Backup function** in the Settings Panel first. This preserves your **Sessions, Configuration, Context Library, and Neural Skills** — the unique personality and knowledge you've built.
