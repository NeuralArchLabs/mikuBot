### ✨ What's New in This Release?

*   **🎭 Kinetic Emoji Engine:** The neural signature is now alive. We've implemented a custom animation dictionary where every emoji follows unique, organic keyframe patterns (shimmer, bounce, pulse) tailored to its visual identity.
*   **👁️ Studio Spoiler Engine:** Hide sensitive information with style. Use `||spoiler||` syntax to wrap text in a premium glassmorphic blur that reveals with a smooth cyan glow only when you hover over it.
*   **🏗️ Variable-Length Fenced Blocks:** Total control over Markdown nesting. MikuBot now supports 4+ backtick fences (```` ````), allowing you to embed complex Markdown examples and triple-backtick blocks without syntax corruption.
*   **🛡️ Universal Escaping System:** We've solved "stray backtick" bugs. Our new 3-phase escaping pipeline robustly handles all special characters (`\_`, `\[`, `\\`), rendering them literally and preventing them from accidentally breaking your formatting.
*   **📚 Modernized Footnotes:** A cleaner reading experience. Footnote markers have been stripped of non-essential borders and prefixes, adopting a minimalist numeric style that aligns with global typography standards.
*   **📐 Proportional Readability Constraint:** Better layout for ultra-wide screens. Chat balloons are now constrained to a maximum width of `4xl` (896px), ensuring ideal line lengths and superior reading comfort on massive monitors.
*   **📊 Premium Mermaid Visualization:** Total technical clarity. MikuBot now renders high-fidelity Mermaid diagrams, supporting everything from simple Flowcharts to complex GitGraphs, ER Diagrams, and Mindmaps with optimized "Studio Elite" scaling.

### 📦 How to Update Safely

Simply download the new installer **MikuBot-Setup-2.2.1.exe** from the **Assets** section below and run it. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Safety First:** The wizard will handle the transition of your workspace folders. However, as this update introduces major rendering changes, we strongly recommend: **Run the Backup function** in the Settings Panel first. This preserves your **Sessions, Configuration, Context Library, and Neural Skills** — the unique personality and knowledge you've built.
